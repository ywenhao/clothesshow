BAO YAO FEI — Bao Yao Fei (The Hague, the Netherlands, 1990) graduated in Fashion and Textile at the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague where he studied under, amongst others, Marina Yee and Jurgi Persoons. After his internship at Christian Wijnants (Antwerp, Belgium) he designed a graduation collection which was a mix between the architectural lines of Le Corbusier and the female form with details of his Chinese heritage. With this collection he won the Beurs voor Top Talent Creatieve Industrie 2012 (Scholarship for ‘Top Talent’ Creative Industries 2012), which enabled him to start his own label BAO YAO FEI.

COLLECTION — With BAO YAO FEI he wants to create a collection full of key pieces that will never go out of style. By redesigning basics with a modern twist, the garments are not bound to a season, making them timeless new classics. The base of the collection consists of black and white garments with an additional colour every half year. In the first collection the additional colour is navy.

 

All pieces are hand crafted in Bao Yao’s studio in The Netherlands.
